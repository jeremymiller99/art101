Pseudocode for making eggs

1. get a pan, put it on the stove and turn it on

2. get butter and eggs from fridge, put butter in the pan

3. crack eggs into the pan

4. season the eggs with salt and pepper

5. get a spatula and mix the eggs

6. when they are done cooking, put on a plate and enjoy

Pseudocode for Tetris

1. Initialize the game board and first tetromino

2. Display the current game board with falling tetromino

3. Accept user input for movement and rotation

4. Automatically move tetromino down over time

5. Check for collisions or if the tetromino has landed

6. Lock the tetromino into place and check for completed lines

7. Clear lines and update the score if needed

8. Spawn a new tetromino

9. Check for game over condition

10. End the game or restart based on player choice
